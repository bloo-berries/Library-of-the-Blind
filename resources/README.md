# Resources

Additional resources, references, and links related to blind and visually impaired communication methodologies.

## Organizations

### International
- **[World Blind Union (WBU)](https://www.worldblindunion.org/)** - Global organization representing blind and partially sighted persons
- **[International Council on English Braille (ICEB)](https://iceb.org/)** - International organization for English Braille standards
- **[International Braille and Technology Center for the Blind](https://www.nfb.org/programs-services/braille-technology-center)** - Technology center providing resources and training

### Regional
- **[American Printing House for the Blind (APH)](https://www.aph.org/)** - United States - Leading provider of educational materials and technology
- **[Canadian National Institute for the Blind (CNIB)](https://www.cnib.ca/)** - Canada - National organization providing services and advocacy
- **[Royal National Institute of Blind People (RNIB)](https://www.rnib.org.uk/)** - United Kingdom - Leading sight loss charity
- **[African Braille Consortium](https://africanbraille.org/)** - Africa - Regional organization promoting Braille across Africa
- **[New Zealand Braille Authority](https://www.brailleauthority.org.nz/)** - New Zealand - National authority for Braille standards
- **[Australian Braille Authority](https://brailleaustralia.org/)** - Australia - National authority for Braille standards

## Standards

### Braille Standards
- **[Unified English Braille (UEB)](https://www.iceb.org/ueb.html)** - International standard for English Braille
- **[Bharati Braille](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bharati_Braille)** - Unified standard for Indian languages
- **[ISO 13125](https://www.iso.org/standard/57164.html)** - Tactile map symbols standard

### Accessibility Standards
- **[WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/quickref/)** - International web accessibility standards
- **[Section 508](https://www.section508.gov/)** - United States accessibility law and standards
- **[EN 301 549](https://www.etsi.org/deliver/etsi_en/301500_301599/301549/)** - European Union accessibility requirements for ICT products and services

## Technology Resources

### Screen Reader Software
- **[NVDA](https://www.nvaccess.org/)** - Free, open-source screen reader for Windows
- **[JAWS](https://www.freedomscientific.com/products/software/jaws/)** - Commercial screen reader for Windows
- **[VoiceOver](https://www.apple.com/accessibility/vision/)** - Built-in Apple screen reader for macOS, iOS, and other Apple platforms
- **[Orca](https://wiki.gnome.org/Projects/Orca)** - Free, open-source screen reader for Linux

### Braille Translation
- **[Liblouis](https://liblouis.org/)** - Open-source Braille translation library
- **[Duxbury Braille Translator](https://www.duxburysystems.com/)** - Commercial Braille translation software

### Braille Display Manufacturers
- **[Freedom Scientific](https://www.freedomscientific.com/)** - Manufacturer of Braille displays and screen readers
- **[HumanWare](https://www.humanware.com/)** - Manufacturer of assistive technology including Braille displays
- **[APH (American Printing House)](https://www.aph.org/)** - Manufacturer of educational and assistive technology products
- **[Orbit Research](https://www.orbitresearch.com/)** - Manufacturer of refreshable Braille displays
