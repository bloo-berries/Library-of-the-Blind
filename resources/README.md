## Standards

### Braille Standards
- **[Unified English Braille (UEB)](https://www.iceb.org/ueb.html)** - International standard for English Braille
- **[Bharati Braille](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bharati_Braille)** - Unified standard for Indian languages
- **[ISO 13125](https://www.iso.org/standard/57164.html)** - Tactile map symbols standard
- **[BANA (Braille Authority of North America)](https://www.brailleauthority.org/)** - USA/Canada Braille standards
- **[UKAAF (UK Association for Accessible Formats)](https://www.ukaaf.org/)** - UK Braille and accessible format standards
- **[ICEB (International Council on English Braille)](https://iceb.org/)** - UEB governance
- **[World Braille Council](https://www.worldblindunion.org/)** - International Braille coordination
- **[European Blind Union standards committee](https://www.euroblind.org/)** - EU Braille coordination

### Accessibility Standards
- **[WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG21/quickref/)** - International web accessibility standards (WCAG 2.2)
- **[Section 508](https://www.section508.gov/)** - United States accessibility law and standards
- **[EN 301 549](https://www.etsi.org/deliver/etsi_en/301500_301599/301549/)** - European Union accessibility requirements for ICT products and services
- **[ADA Title III](https://www.ada.gov/)** - United States - Public accommodation accessibility
- **[Equality Act 2010](https://www.legislation.gov.uk/ukpga/2010/15/contents)** - United Kingdom - Disability provisions
- **[AODA](https://www.ontario.ca/laws/statute/05a11)** - Canada (Ontario) - Provincial accessibility law
- **[Disability Discrimination Act](https://www.legislation.gov.au/Details/C2014C00013)** - Australia - National disability law
- **[JIS X 8341](https://www.jisc.go.jp/)** - Japan - ICT accessibility standard
- **[KS X 9211](https://www.standard.go.kr/)** - South Korea - Web accessibility standard
- **[GB/T 37668](https://www.sac.gov.cn/)** - China - Web accessibility standard
- **[ISO 24751](https://www.iso.org/standard/43614.html)** - Individualized adaptability and accessibility
- **[ISO 14289 (PDF/UA)](https://www.iso.org/standard/64599.html)** - Accessible PDF standard

### International Frameworks and Treaties
- **[Marrakesh Treaty](https://www.wipo.int/treaties/en/ip/marrakesh/)** - Copyright exception for accessible formats
- **[UN CRPD (Convention on Rights of Persons with Disabilities)](https://www.un.org/development/desa/disabilities/convention-on-the-rights-of-persons-with-disabilities.html)** - International disability rights treaty

## Technology Resources

### Screen Reader Software
- **[NVDA](https://www.nvaccess.org/)** - Free, open-source screen reader for Windows
- **[JAWS](https://www.freedomscientific.com/products/software/jaws/)** - Commercial screen reader for Windows
- **[VoiceOver](https://www.apple.com/accessibility/vision/)** - Built-in Apple screen reader for macOS, iOS, and other Apple platforms
- **[Orca](https://wiki.gnome.org/Projects/Orca)** - Free, open-source screen reader for Linux

### Braille Translation
- **[Liblouis](https://liblouis.org/)** - Open-source Braille translation library
- **[Duxbury Braille Translator](https://www.duxburysystems.com/)** - Commercial Braille translation software

### Braille Display Manufacturers
- **[Freedom Scientific](https://www.freedomscientific.com/)** - Manufacturer of Braille displays and screen readers
- **[HumanWare](https://www.humanware.com/)** - Manufacturer of assistive technology including Braille displays
- **[APH (American Printing House)](https://www.aph.org/)** - Manufacturer of educational and assistive technology products
- **[Orbit Research](https://www.orbitresearch.com/)** - Manufacturer of refreshable Braille displays
